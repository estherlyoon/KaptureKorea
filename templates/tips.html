Fast Facts


First thing's first, here are some important things you need to know before going to South Korea:

Capital: Seoul

Currency: Won (1 USD = 1181 KRW)

President: Moon Jae-in
Government: Republic
Population: 51 million
South Korea has the world's fastest internet connection speeds!


History


The history of Korea, which split into North and South relatively recently, in 1945, is rich and detailed. While most probably think of the aforementioned divide, beauty produdcts, and the booming entertainment industry, there are millenia of political systems, religious influences, power struggles, and more that have shaped Korean culture as it is today. While an exhaustive account of the changes that occurred in the Korean peninsula won't directly reside on this site, below lies a selection of fantastic resources that you should use to learn about the country you're soon to be traversing.
